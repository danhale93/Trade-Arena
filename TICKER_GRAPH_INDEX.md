## ✨ Key Points

### What is the Ticker Graph?
A **real-time animated chart** showing each bot's cumulative P&L performance as they trade.

### Where is it?
Below the bot grid, above the trade log. In the app, look for: **📊 BOT PERFORMANCE TICKER**

### How does it work?
1. Bot completes trade
2. P&L recorded automatically
3. Chart updates in real-time
4. Legend shows statistics
5. You can monitor all bots at once

### What does it show?
- **Each bot's line** (different colors)
- **Cumulative profit/loss** (vertical position)
- **Win rate %** (legend statistics)
- **Trade progression** (horizontal time)

### Is it fast?
Yes! 60 FPS smooth animation, <10% CPU impact

### Do I need to configure anything?
No! Works out of the box. Optional customization available.
