## 🎓 Understanding the Numbers

### TRADES/MIN Counter
- **What it is**: Count of trades in the last 60 seconds
- **Update frequency**: Every trade (1-5 per second)
- **Expected value with 6 bots**: 300-900
- **Peak**: Around 900-1000 (then resets)
- **Why it drops**: Old trades older than 60 seconds get removed

### Trade Delay
- **Range**: 400-1200 milliseconds
- **Why randomized**: Prevents bot synchronization
- **Effect**: Natural, organic trading pattern
- **Result**: ~75 trades per minute per bot
