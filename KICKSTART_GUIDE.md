# 🚀 Kickstart Your Trading Capital: The $20 Guide

Welcome to Trade Arena! To help you get started with gas, slippage, and your first betting balance, we've compiled a list of reputable AI tasks, faucets, and quests that can help you earn your first $20+ on the Base network.

## 1. Coinbase Learning Rewards ($20 - $40)
The fastest way to get real capital. If you have a Coinbase account, you can earn various tokens (including USDC) by completing short educational modules.
- **Task:** Watch 1-minute videos and answer a 1-question quiz.
- **Reward:** Typically $3 - $10 per module.
- **Network:** You can send these rewards to your Base wallet.
- **Link:** [Coinbase Learning](https://www.coinbase.com/learning-rewards)

## 2. Alaya AI: Data Labeling for AI (Earn as you work)
Alaya AI is a decentralized data labeling platform where you can earn crypto by helping train AI models.
- **Task:** Label images, text, and audio to improve AI accuracy.
- **Reward:** Tokens based on task volume and accuracy.
- **Network:** Multiple, including Base-compatible ecosystems.
- **Link:** [Alaya AI](https://www.alaya-ai.com/)

## 3. Layer3 & Galxe: Base Ecosystem Quests ($5 - $10)
Complete onboarding tasks for the Base network to earn rewards.
- **Task:** Follow projects on social media, swap small amounts, or bridge tokens.
- **Reward:** Tokens, NFTs, and XP that often lead to airdrops.
- **Link:** [Layer3 Base Quests](https://layer3.xyz/base) | [Galxe Base Space](https://galxe.com/base)

## 4. Base Official Faucets (Gas Money)
If you just need a tiny bit of ETH for gas to get your first trade through:
- **Base Sepolia Faucet:** [Base Faucet](https://www.base.org/faucets) (For testnet simulation)
- **Bungee Refuel:** Great for moving tiny amounts of gas from other chains to Base.

## 5. In-App Task Center Rewards ($50 Arena Credits)
Don't forget to claim your starting capital directly in the Trade Arena!
- **Claim Faucet:** $50 in virtual credits to test your strategies.
- **Social Tasks:** Follow us on Twitter/X and join Discord for more rewards.

---

### Tips for Success:
- **Gas Management:** Base is very cheap, but always keep ~$1 of ETH for gas.
- **Slippage:** Set your slippage tolerance correctly (0.5% - 1%) in the bot settings to ensure trades execute smoothly.
- **Security:** Never share your private keys or seed phrases. Trade Arena uses Privy for secure, seedless onboarding.
